Percentage Calculator 1 - Kaise Use Karen

1. "Total Value" me apna poora number dalo (जैसे 1000)
2. "Percentage" me wo percentage dalo jo nikalna hai (जैसे 20)
3. Calculate button dabao, result dikhega!

Percentage Calculator 2 - Kaise Use Karen

1. "Original Value" me purani value dalo (जैसे 500)
2. "New Value" me nayi value dalo (जैसे 600)
3. Calculate button dabao, percentage badhao/kam dikhega!

KG/Gram Calculator - Kaise Use Karen

1. "Price per KG" me ek kg ka dam dalo (जैसे 100)
2. "Quantity" me apni matra dalo
3. Choose KG ya Gram selector se
4. Calculate dabao, total price dikhega!
